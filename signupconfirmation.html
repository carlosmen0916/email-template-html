important Information
Passenger's Responsibilities
Arrive at the pickup spot on time. Be ready and prepared for the journey. Keep the contact information of the driver handy.
Driver's Responsibilities
Arrive at the pickup spot on time. Be ready and prepared for the journey. Keep the contact information of the driver handy.
Communication
The driver should inform the passenger in case of any delay that exceeds the grace period. The passenger should contact the driver through the Movelz chat provided in the platform. Both parties should maintain effective communication to coordinate the pickup process.
Grace Period and Waiting Time
The driver should aim to arrive at the pickup spot before or exactly at the scheduled time. A 10-minute grace period is provided to accommodate minor delays or unforeseen circumstances. If the driver arrives within the grace period, the passenger should wait at the pickup spot. The driver should make every effort to minimize waiting time for the passenger.
Alternate Arrangements
If the driver is unable to make it within the grace period, alternative arrangements should be discussed. The passenger and driver can agree on a new pickup location or reschedule the pickup time, if feasible. In case of extended delays or unavailability of the driver, the passenger may consider alternate transportation options.
